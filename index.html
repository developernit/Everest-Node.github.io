About us

We are small but dedicated group of people from different parts of the world, the purpose of starting orchestrator was to dip the toes into the world of crypto but in the end we jumped all in. At the beginning, the investment seed for orchestrator stake was managed personally and the transcoding server run locally in EU but after realising the potential of livepeer for the future of video infrastructure, we started to expland rapidly. Starting with stake of 10 LPT about 6months ago, we managed to accumulate 180 LPT by the date of March 2022 and servers to handle transcoding service in almost all continents. We have accumulated total of 1.44 ETH from transcoding in L1 which has been reinvested into expansion but if you stake with us, the part of eth fees accumulated will be rewarded to you on the basis of reward cut percentage.

Follow the link to livepeer explorer to see more details of the orchestrator node and stake from explorer safely. explorer.livepeer.org

Benefits of staking with us

Rewards

We have a fixed reward and fee cut percentage of 0% and 90% respectively.

This means whatever we earn from transcoding in livepeer network you get a cut of 10% in fees and 100% of the reward from inflation which will be called daily.
Transparency

We have a dashboard to view the transcoding stats on the server

You will be able to login to a transcoder dashboard to see the stats of the transcoding server as it accumulates ticket and transcodes streams for fee rewards.
